**Ownership**: Growth/Operations Lead (not engineers)

---

## STRATEGIC APPROACH

### Why This Matters

**Without experts, the platform is a graveyard.**

Most platforms fail not at launch but at the second user. Parents sign up, see no experts, leave. Never return.

Solution: Handpick first 100 experts. Recruit them. Verify them. Have them live on Day 1.

This creates two effects:
1. **Social proof** - Parents see real experts, trust platform
2. **Network effect** - Experts refer other experts
