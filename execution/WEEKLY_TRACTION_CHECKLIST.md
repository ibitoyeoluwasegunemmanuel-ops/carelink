## When You Fall Behind (What to Do)

**If Expert Recruitment Falls Behind**
- Increase outreach (contact more per day)
- Make more follow-up calls
- Prioritize quick wins (associations)
- Ask existing experts for referrals
- Lower bar slightly if needed (more careful verification)

**If Parent Signups Fall Behind**
- Increase social media promotion
- Reach out to partner networks
- Referral program (incentivize existing parents)
- Influencer outreach
- Direct email campaigns

**If Discussions Fall Behind**
- Community lead seeds more manually
- Ask experts to participate
- Partner communities cross-promote
- Daily discussion prompts

**If Partnerships Fall Behind**
- More aggressive outreach
- Lower tier threshold (accept more Tier 3)
- Faster sales cycle (less negotiation)
- Internal team as partners (staff as experts)
- Fast-track 5-7 instead of 15
