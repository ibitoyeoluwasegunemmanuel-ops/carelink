# Customer Discovery Interview Scripts

**Purpose**: Validate market opportunity and understand real professional/parent challenges before building features

**Approach**: Conversational discovery, not sales pitch. Listen more than talk.

---

## EXPERT DISCOVERY INTERVIEW (15-20 minutes)

**Goal**: Understand how they currently serve families, what's hard about it, and whether a platform could help

### Opening (1 minute)

```
Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time to chat. 

I'm building CareLink Africa - a platform connecting families with 
verified professionals like you. I'm not here to sell anything - 
I'm just trying to understand how professionals actually work with 
families and where the biggest gaps are.

Would you have 15 minutes to chat? It'll help me build something 
actually useful.
```

### Current Practice (4 minutes)

Ask about their current reality:

1. **Volume & Demand**
   - "How many families contact you in a month for new consultations?"
   - "How do they typically find you?" (referral, Google, clinic, word-of-mouth)
   - "Do you have capacity to take on more families?"

2. **Challenges**
   - "What's the hardest part about reaching families who need your help?"
   - "How much time do you spend on administrative work vs. actual therapy?"
   - "Do families ever struggle to find the right expert?"

Listen for pain points: Too many inquiries? Not enough? Hard to manage? Time-consuming?

### Platform Validation (6 minutes)

3. **Discovery Problem**
   - "If a trusted platform had 100+ verified professionals, would families be more confident finding you?"
   - "Would you be interested in being featured on a verified expert directory?"
   - "What would need to be true for you to join?"

4. **Verification & Trust**
   - "How do you currently prove your credentials to families?"
   - "Would you be willing to verify your credentials (takes 10 minutes) to get featured?"
   - "What verification methods matter most to you?"

5. **Parent Referrals**
   - "If we sent you families actively looking for [your specialty], would that be valuable?"
   - "How would you prefer to receive referrals?" (email, dashboard, WhatsApp)
   - "What percentage of referrals typically convert to actual clients?"

Listen for: Is this actually helpful? Would they use it? What's the barrier?

### Objections & Barriers (3 minutes)

6. **Deal Breakers**
   - "What would make you NOT want to join a platform like this?"
   - "Have you tried similar platforms before? What didn't work?"
   - "What's your biggest concern about joining CareLink?"

Listen for: Exclusivity requirements? Payment models? Liability? Time commitment?

### Close (1 minute)

```
This has been really helpful. Based on our conversation, I think 
CareLink could actually help you reach more families.

Would you be interested in being one of the first 100 verified 
experts when we launch in June? No pressure at all - just wanted 
to see if you were interested.
```

## PARENT DISCOVERY INTERVIEW (15-20 minutes)

**Goal**: Understand how parents search for help, what information they need, and what would make them trust a platform

### Opening (1 minute)

```
Hi [Name], thanks for talking with me. 

I'm building a platform to help families with children who have 
special needs connect with verified experts and other parents. 
I'm trying to understand what you actually need most.

Do you have 15 minutes?
```

### Discovery Journey (5 minutes)

Ask about their path to finding help:

1. **Diagnosis & Initial Confusion**
   - "When your child was diagnosed, what was the hardest part?"
   - "How did you start looking for expert help?"
   - "Where did you search first?" (Google, Facebook, family referrals, clinic)

2. **Trust & Verification**
   - "How did you know if professionals were actually qualified?"
   - "Did you ever hire someone who wasn't qualified or was a bad fit?"
   - "What would make you trust a professional immediately?"

3. **Information Gaps**
   - "What information did you wish you had when your child was first diagnosed?"
   - "Did you find reliable resources about [condition]?"
   - "Did you end up self-teaching from random sources?"

Listen for: Where are they currently finding help? How much time do they spend searching? Do they trust what they find?

### Platform Validation (5 minutes)

4. **Expert Directory**
   - "If there was a place with 100+ verified experts you could browse and book, would that help?"
   - "Would you prefer calling, emailing, or booking through a platform?"
   - "What information about an expert would make you confident hiring them?"

5. **Community & Knowledge**
   - "Do you talk to other parents who have similar situations?"
   - "Where do those conversations happen?" (WhatsApp groups, Facebook, friend groups)
   - "What would make you want to join a parent community?"

6. **Resources & Education**
   - "What resources have been most helpful?" (articles, videos, support groups)
   - "Do you trust medical/professional content?"
   - "Would expert-written articles be more helpful than random blog posts?"

Listen for: Is there actual demand? What format works? Would they actually use it?

### Barriers & Concerns (3 minutes)

7. **Deal Breakers**
   - "What would make you NOT trust a platform like this?"
   - "Are you concerned about privacy/data sharing?"
   - "Would you be willing to share your location or condition info?"

Listen for: Trust issues? Privacy concerns? Cost? Complexity?

### Close (1 minute)

```
This has been incredibly helpful. I think we're building something 
that could actually make your life easier.

Would you be interested in joining early when we launch? You'd get 
access to experts and a community of parents going through the same 
thing. No cost - we're just trying to get it right.

Can I follow up with you next week?
```

## KEY LISTENING SKILLS

**What to Look For**:
- Enthusiasm: Do they light up talking about it?
- Friction: Where do they pause or hesitate?
- Specificity: Do they give concrete examples?
- Time: How much time do they currently spend on this problem?

**Red Flags** (Platform may not be needed):
- "I don't really have a problem with this"
- "I've tried other platforms and they didn't work"
- "Too much trouble to verify my credentials"
- "Families find me fine already"

**Green Lights** (Market opportunity):
- "This would save me so much time"
- "I wish there was something like this"
- "I'd definitely use it if [specific feature]"
- "Can you launch faster?"

---

## Post-Interview Steps

1. **Immediately after**: Log conversation in Tracking Sheet
2. **Within 24 hours**: Send follow-up email with next steps
3. **If interested**: Schedule deeper conversation (week 2)
4. **If objection**: Document and iterate messaging

---

## Success Metrics from Interviews

Count:
- Total conversations completed
- Professionals expressing interest
- Parents wanting early access
- Specific pain points mentioned 3+ times
- Volunteer advocates who want to help

Do NOT count:
- People who were polite
- People who said "maybe"
- People who didn't object

Interest = "Yes, I would use this" or action taken (signed up, shared contact)
