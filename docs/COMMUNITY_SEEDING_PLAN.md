# CareLink Africa - Community Seeding Plan

**Goal**: Launch with active, engaged community  
**Targets**: 100 first users, 50 discussions, 5 active moderators  
**Timeline**: Weeks 5-8 (launch preparation phase)  
**Philosophy**: Seed community with real people and real discussions, not fake activity  

---

## Community Seeding Overview

### Why Seed Community?

**The Empty Platform Problem**
- New platforms look empty and useless
- Users avoid platforms with no activity
- Discussions need context to be interesting
- First discussions set tone for community

**Seeding Strategy**
- Find real early adopters (parents, experts, advocates)
- Create authentic discussions that parents need
- Get moderators invested in success
- Build momentum before public launch

### Discussion Seeding Process

#### Step 1: Create Discussions (Week 6)

```
For each discussion:
☐ Write compelling topic title
☐ Write opening post (200-300 words)
  - Hook: Why this matters
  - Context: What we're discussing
  - Invite: "Share your experience"
  - Question: Specific thing you want input on
☐ Add expert perspective (expert quote/tip)
☐ Post discussion
☐ Schedule for visibility
```

**Example Opening Post**

```
Title: "What were your first signs? How did you get diagnosed?"

Opening:
"Every parent's journey is different. Some notice early signs 
at 12 months, others at 3 years. Some had an easy diagnosis, 
others had to fight to be heard.

We'd love to hear your story. What were the early signs you 
noticed? How long did it take to get a diagnosis? What advice 
do you have for parents just starting this journey?

Share your experience - it might help someone else."

Expert Input (from speech therapist):
"From a professional perspective, look for: delayed speech, 
lack of eye contact, repetitive movements, difficulty with 
transitions. Every child is different, but early intervention 
makes a huge difference."
```

#### Step 2: Seed Initial Comments (Week 6)

```
Don't leave discussions empty!
└─ Add 2-3 authentic responses to each thread
  - Share realistic parent experiences
  - Include expert perspectives
  - Ask follow-up questions
  - Model good engagement
```

**Response Examples**

```
From "Parent Advocate 1":
"We noticed our son wasn't making eye contact around 14 months. 
His pediatrician thought he was fine, but we pushed for an 
assessment. Best decision we made. It took 6 months for full 
diagnosis, but we got early intervention started."

From "Speech Therapist - Expert":
"Early intervention is key. Every 6 months of delay in the first 
3 years matters. If you have concerns, get assessed. It's free in 
many places and can change everything."

From "Parent Advocate 2":
"Our experience: pediatrician first, then developmental pediatrician, 
then speech/OT evaluations. It was a process but worth it."
```

#### Step 3: Encourage Real Engagement (Week 7)

```
Once discussions seeded:
├─ Invite real parents to join
├─ Ask experts to answer questions
├─ Highlight interesting responses
├─ Thank engaged participants
└─ Model good community culture
```

## Phase 3: Recruit & Develop Community Moderators (Weeks 6-8)

### What is a Moderator?

```
Role: Guide and nurture community discussions
├─ Welcome new members
├─ Answer questions
├─ Flag inappropriate content
├─ Highlight good discussions
├─ Set tone and culture
└─ Report to moderation team
```

## Community Culture Guide

### Core Values

**Respectful**
- We listen and learn from each other
- Different perspectives are welcome
- Disagreements are managed kindly

**Supportive**
- We're here to help each other
- Celebrating wins together
- Supporting through challenges

**Honest**
- Real experiences shared
- No judgment or pressure
- Practical advice given

**Safe**
- Confidentiality respected
- Privacy protected
- No advertising or exploitation

### Community Guidelines (Simple)

```
Be Kind
├─ No judgment or shaming
├─ Recognize diverse approaches
└─ Support each other

Be Honest
├─ Share real experiences
├─ Ask genuine questions
└─ Give practical advice

Be Respectful
├─ Listen to others
├─ Respect different views
├─ No discrimination

Be Safe
├─ No personal attacks
├─ No spam or ads
├─ Protect privacy

Be Helpful
├─ Share resources
├─ Answer questions
├─ Celebrate successes
```
