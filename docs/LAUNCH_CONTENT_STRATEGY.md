# CareLink Africa - Launch Content Strategy

**Goal**: Launch with 50 high-quality articles, 20 FAQs, and 20 community discussion starters  
**Timeline**: Weeks 2-7 (5 weeks to create/curate content)  
**Focus**: Parent-focused, practical, trustworthy  

---

## Content Philosophy

**Content serves three purposes:**
1. **Attract** - Parents searching for help find us in Google
2. **Educate** - Help families understand conditions and support
3. **Engage** - Community discussions build loyalty

**Quality > Quantity**
- Every article must be well-written and useful
- Every article must be fact-checked
- Every article must reflect expert consensus
- Plagiarism check every piece

## Content Quality Standards

### Accuracy
```
☐ All claims fact-checked
☐ Sources cited
☐ Expert reviewed
☐ No medical claims without evidence
☐ No substitute for professional advice disclaimer
```

### Clarity
```
☐ Written for parent audience (not medical jargon)
☐ Clear structure with headers
☐ Short paragraphs
☐ Actionable advice
☐ Real-world examples
```

### Tone
```
☐ Warm and empathetic
☐ Non-judgmental
☐ Hopeful but realistic
☐ Inclusive
☐ Respectful of all conditions/families
```

### Completeness
```
☐ Includes practical strategies
☐ Includes expert perspective
☐ Includes resources for more info
☐ Includes where to get help
☐ Includes common questions addressed
```
