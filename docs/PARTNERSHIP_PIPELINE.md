# CareLink Africa - Partnership Pipeline

**Goal**: Establish 10-15 strategic partnerships by launch for referrals, credibility, and reach  
**Focus**: Nigeria first  
**Timeline**: Weeks 2-8  

---

## Partnership Strategy Overview

### Why Partnerships Matter

**Credibility**: NGOs/hospitals endorsement = trust for parents  
**Reach**: Access to their networks (1,000s of families)  
**Referrals**: They recommend CareLink to clients/patients  
**Content**: Co-create resources with experts  
**Volunteers**: Moderators, experts, ambassadors  
**Funding**: Potential sponsorships or grants  

### Partnership Types

```
Tier 1: Strategic Partners (Must-Have)
├─ 3-5 large NGOs
├─ 2-3 hospital networks
├─ 1-2 government/policy bodies
└─ Impact: Major credibility & reach

Tier 2: Content Partners (Important)
├─ 3-5 expert organizations
├─ 2-3 educational institutions
└─ Impact: Authority & content

Tier 3: Community Partners (Nice-to-Have)
├─ 5-10 local organizations
├─ 3-5 parent groups
└─ Impact: Grassroots reach

Total Target: 15+ partnerships by launch
```

#### Outreach Process

```
Step 1: Research (Week 2)
├─ Identify 10-15 potential NGO partners
├─ Research leadership
├─ Find connection points
├─ Analyze fit

Step 2: Initial Contact (Week 3)
├─ Send introduction email
├─ Personalized (specific name, specific reason)
├─ Request 20-min call
└─ Response window: 1 week

Step 3: Discovery Call (Week 4)
├─ Introduce CareLink mission
├─ Understand their needs
├─ Explore potential synergies
├─ Propose partnership idea
└─ Ask for next steps

Step 4: Partnership Discussion (Week 5)
├─ Share partner benefits doc
├─ Discuss specific collaboration
├─ Agree on details
├─ Formalize (simple letter)

Step 5: Collaboration Begins (Week 6)
├─ Regular communication starts
├─ Co-create content/plans
├─ Joint announcements
├─ Build relationship
```

**Sample Partnership Email**

```
Subject: Partnership Opportunity - CareLink Africa

Hi [Director Name],

I'm reaching out because we admire [Organization]'s work supporting 
special needs families in Nigeria.

We're launching CareLink Africa - a platform connecting families with 
verified experts and resources. We'd love to partner with [Organization] 
to amplify our impact together.

Potential collaboration:
- Co-created content for your members
- Referral pathway for families
- Joint expert directory
- Shared webinars and training

Would you be open to a 20-minute call to explore this?

Best regards,
[Your Name]
CareLink Africa
```

## Risk Management

### Partnership Risks

**Risk 1: Partners go inactive**
```
Mitigation:
├─ Clear agreements & expectations
├─ Monthly check-ins
├─ Quarterly reviews
├─ Renewal conversations
└─ Backup partners identified
```

**Risk 2: Alignment issues**
```
Mitigation:
├─ Clear discussion at start
├─ Regular communication
├─ Transparent about issues
├─ Adjust collaboration as needed
└─ End partnership respectfully if needed
```

**Risk 3: Partner doesn't deliver**
```
Mitigation:
├─ Clear deliverables defined
├─ Timeline with milestones
├─ Regular check-ins
├─ Support if struggling
└─ Realistic expectations
```
