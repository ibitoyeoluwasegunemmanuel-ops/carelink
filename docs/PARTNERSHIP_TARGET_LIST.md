## Risk Management

### Risk 1: Partners Go Inactive Post-Launch

```
Mitigation:
├─ Clear expectations in agreement
├─ Monthly check-in calls
├─ Track referral metrics
├─ Celebrate successes
├─ Support them in collaboration
└─ Renewal conversations at month 3
```

### Risk 2: Low Referral Traffic from Partners

```
Mitigation:
├─ Make referral easy (simple link/button)
├─ Provide partners with marketing materials
├─ Give them login access to see referrals
├─ Offer co-branded materials
├─ Create incentive (featured, highlighted)
└─ Regular communication about benefits
```

### Risk 3: Difficult Partner Relationships

```
Mitigation:
├─ Regular communication
├─ Clear expectations upfront
├─ Respectful disagreement resolution
├─ Flexibility on collaboration details
├─ Professional relationship management
└─ Know when to politely end if misaligned
```

### Risk 4: Not Reaching 15 Partnerships

```
Contingency:
├─ If on pace for 12-14: Add 2-3 community partners
├─ If on pace for 10-12: Deepen existing partnerships
├─ If on pace for <10: Launch with what have + highlight quality
├─ Quality > Quantity - 10 strong partnerships > 15 weak ones
└─ Plan: Reach out for more in month 2
```

### Risk 5: NGO/Hospital Partnerships Don't Prioritize

```
Mitigation:
├─ Make collaboration easy & lightweight
├─ Assign single point of contact (not bureaucracy)
├─ Provide pre-made marketing materials
├─ Offer recurring revenue share if possible (phase 2)
├─ Show them impact & numbers
└─ Celebrate their contribution publicly
```
