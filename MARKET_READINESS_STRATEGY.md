## Key Principles

### 1. **Quality > Quantity**
- 50 great articles > 500 mediocre articles
- 100 verified experts > 1,000 unverified
- 5 excellent moderators > 50 inactive ones
- Real discussions > bot-generated threads

### 2. **Authenticity > Perfection**
- Real parent voices > polished marketing
- Organic engagement > artificial activity
- Genuine expert input > solo company content
- Community culture > feature completeness

### 3. **Trust > Growth**
- Verified experts matter more than expert count
- Expert endorsement beats marketing claims
- Community recommendations beat ads
- Demonstrated support beats promised features

### 4. **Substance > Seeding**
- Real articles people search for
- Real discussions parents want
- Real experts families trust
- Real engagement that converts

### 5. **Preparation > Speed**
- Week spent on experts = better quality
- Time on content = organic search traffic
- Effort on partnerships = credibility
- Thought on community = better culture
