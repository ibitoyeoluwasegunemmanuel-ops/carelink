## Research Instructions

1. **LinkedIn Search**:
   - Search: "Speech Therapist Lagos Nigeria"
   - Filter: 500+ connections = established professionals
   - Copy name, title, organization, profile URL

2. **Professional Associations**:
   - Speech, Language & Hearing Association of Nigeria (SLHAN)
   - Occupational Therapy Association of Nigeria
   - Nigerian Psychological Association
   - Extract member directories with contact info

3. **Hospital Directories**:
   - Major hospitals in Lagos: LUTH, Reddington, Eko Hospital
   - Major hospitals in Abuja: ABUTH, Cedarcrest Hospital
   - Major hospitals in Port Harcourt: UPTH, Trinity Hospital
   - Contact HR/specialist departments

4. **Google Maps/Reviews**:
   - Search "Speech Therapy Lagos"
   - Search "Occupational Therapy Abuja"
   - Search "Child Psychology Port Harcourt"
   - Extract clinic names and contact numbers

5. **University Faculty**:
   - University of Lagos (Speech & Hearing)
   - Abubauja University (Allied Health)
   - University of Port Harcourt (Health Sciences)
   - Contact faculty/lecturers
