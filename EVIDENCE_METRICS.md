# Evidence Metrics - What Counts As Traction

**Principle**: Only count real market signals. Not effort. Not output. Not engagement with inanimate objects.

---

## PRIMARY TRACTION METRICS

### EXPERT CONVERSATIONS (Demand Signal #1)

**What counts**:
- ✅ Expert replied to your contact message (email, call, WhatsApp)
- ✅ Expert agreed to a 20-minute discovery conversation
- ✅ Expert asked specific questions about the platform
- ✅ Expert expressed interest in being featured
- ✅ Expert committed to verification process
- ✅ Expert completed verification and is now "Verified Expert"

**What doesn't count**:
- ❌ Email sent (no reply yet)
- ❌ Professional said "sounds interesting" with no follow-up action
- ❌ Message delivered to contact
- ❌ Contact viewed your LinkedIn message
- ❌ Professional said "maybe, ask me later"

**Weekly Target**: 5-10 expert conversations completed

---

### PARENT SIGNUPS (Demand Signal #2)

**What counts**:
- ✅ Parent filled out waitlist form on `/waitlist` with valid email
- ✅ Parent joined WhatsApp/Facebook group you created
- ✅ Parent responded to your outreach (yes, they want early access)
- ✅ Parent referred another parent to the platform
- ✅ Parent asked when the platform launches

**What doesn't count**:
- ❌ You shared the link (they didn't click)
- ❌ Landing page impression
- ❌ WhatsApp group created (but no members added)
- ❌ Parent said "I'll think about it"
- ❌ Parent viewed the waitlist form but didn't submit

**Weekly Target**: 5-10 parent signups

---

### PARTNERSHIP ENGAGEMENT (Demand Signal #3)

**What counts**:
- ✅ Partner organization replied to partnership inquiry email
- ✅ Decision-maker agreed to a 30-minute discovery call
- ✅ Partner asked follow-up questions about collaboration
- ✅ Partner committed to a trial/pilot period
- ✅ Partner and you signed partnership agreement
- ✅ Partner agrees to co-promote or co-create content

**What doesn't count**:
- ❌ Email sent to partner (no reply yet)
- ❌ Partner said "sounds good, let me know when you launch"
- ❌ Call requested but not yet scheduled
- ❌ Partner said "maybe" with no timeline
- ❌ Cold email delivered

**Weekly Target**: 1-2 partnership conversations, 1 scheduled meeting

## CONVERSION RATES (Efficiency Metrics)

Track these to understand your effectiveness:

### Expert Recruitment Funnel
```
Experts Contacted → 30
├─ % Who Replied: [#/30] (Target: 30-40%)
├─ % Who Had Conversation: [#/30] (Target: 20-30%)
├─ % Who Expressed Interest: [#/30] (Target: 15-20%)
├─ % Who Verified: [#/30] (Target: 10-15%)
└─ Conversion Rate: [#/30] = [%]
```

**Interpretation**:
- If only 10% reply = messaging or channel needs work
- If 40% reply but only 5% interested = script or pitch needs work
- If interested but don't verify = barrier to verification (too hard, fear, time)

### Parent Outreach Funnel
```
Parents Contacted → 100
├─ % Who Responded: [#/100] (Target: 30-50%)
├─ % Who Signed Up: [#/100] (Target: 15-25%)
└─ Conversion Rate: [#/100] = [%]
```

**Interpretation**:
- If only 10% respond = wrong channels or poor messaging
- If 50% respond but only 5% sign up = they're interested but hesitant (trust issue? complexity?)

### Partnership Pipeline
```
Organizations Contacted → 3
├─ % Who Replied: [#/3] (Target: 50%+)
├─ % Who Agreed to Meeting: [#/3] (Target: 50%+)
└─ Conversion Rate: [#/3] = [%]
```

## RED FLAGS (Stop and Adjust)

If any of these happen, change approach immediately:

🚩 **Expert Outreach**:
- Less than 20% reply rate after 10 contacts
- 0% interest after 5 conversations
- Same objection from 3+ people

🚩 **Parent Acquisition**:
- Less than 10% signup rate after 20 contacts
- No one joining community/WhatsApp group
- Parents saying "not helpful" or "don't need"

🚩 **Partnership**:
- 0 replies after 5 outreach emails
- Partners saying "we don't have time for this"
- Partners asking for payment

---

## GREEN FLAGS (Accelerate)

These signals mean you're onto something:

✅ **Expert Enthusiasm**:
- Expert responds within hours
- Expert volunteers to help recruit others
- Expert asks when they can start

✅ **Parent Demand**:
- Parent signs up within 5 minutes of hearing about it
- Parent shares with other parents
- Parent asks "when do you launch"

✅ **Partnership Momentum**:
- Partner responds quickly and positively
- Partner brings ideas/suggestions
- Partner asks what they need to do

---

## What NOT to Report

❌ **Vanity Metrics** (Do NOT count these):
- "We sent 50 emails" (sending ≠ traction)
- "Landing page got 200 visits" (visiting ≠ commitment)
- "Twitter got 10 likes" (engagement ≠ business)
- "Slack group has 15 people" (size ≠ activity)
- "Database has 100 potential contacts" (list ≠ reached)

❌ **Activity Metrics** (Do NOT report):
- "We wrote the customer discovery script"
- "We created the tracking sheet"
- "We updated the CRM columns"
- "We built a new form"

✅ **Real Traction** (DO report):
- "5 professionals expressed interest"
- "12 parents signed up"
- "2 partnerships scheduled meetings"
- "The biggest pain point is X"
