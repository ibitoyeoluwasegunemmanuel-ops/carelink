## RED FLAGS (Stop & Reassess)

🚩 **TRACK A:**
- Database migration fails
- API endpoints not responding
- Forms don't connect to database
- > 2 critical bugs found in testing
- > 1 day behind schedule

🚩 **TRACK B:**
- < 10 responses by Wednesday
- < 5 interested by Thursday
- < 5 verified by Friday
- Experts canceling after commitment
- Banking/payment info can't be collected

🚩 **COMBINED:**
- Marketplace launch prevented by either track
- Either track completely blocked
- Timeline slipped > 1 day

**If any red flag occurs: Stop. Emergency sync. Replan. Do not proceed.**
